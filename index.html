Our Story

Bringing advanced Zerona® Laser body contouring to East Texas.

My journey with the Zerona® Laser began in 2021 when I joined Austin Body Contouring as a body contouring laser technician. Over the next two years, I received hands-on training and gained extensive experience helping clients achieve their body goals through non-invasive body contouring treatments.

During my time there, I not only witnessed the incredible results clients experienced—I experienced the benefits of Zerona® myself. I had the opportunity to work with hundreds of individuals who were looking to lose inches and stubborn fat, improve their confidence, tighten loose skin, and minimize the appearance of scars and stretch marks. Watching clients transform physically and emotionally was one of the most rewarding parts of my career.

Every client's journey was unique, but one thing remained consistent: the confidence that came from seeing real results. Those experiences reinforced my passion for helping people feel comfortable, confident, and empowered in their own skin.

After welcoming my son, I made the decision to move back to my hometown of Lufkin to be closer to family and build the next chapter of my life. While I was excited to return home, I knew there was one thing missing—access to the advanced body contouring technology that had made such a positive impact on so many lives.

That's why I partnered to bring the revolutionary Zerona® Laser to East Texas. My goal is simple: to provide the people of Lufkin and the surrounding communities with access to the same innovative, non-invasive treatment that I saw change so many lives during my years in the industry.

At Lufkin Body Contouring, we're committed to helping our clients look and feel their best through personalized care, education, and proven technology.

Because confidence isn't about becoming someone else—it's about becoming the best version of yourself. ✨

What is Zerona Laser?

Zerona is a non-invasive laser body contouring treatment that uses low-level laser energy to emulsify fat from fat cells, allowing your body to naturally eliminate it over time. It is painless, relaxing, and requires no downtime.

Benefits

  • Non-invasive body contouring
  • No downtime or recovery needed
  • Helps reduce fat in targeted areas
  • Supports inch loss and body shaping
  • Relaxing, spa-like experience
